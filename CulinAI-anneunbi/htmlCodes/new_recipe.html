LEMON SHRIMP PASTA
8 ounces of pasta
1 pound of large shrimp
3 cloves of garlic
2 tablespoons of olive oil
2 tablespoons of butter
Juice of 1 lemon
1/4 cup of chopped fresh parsley
Salt and pepper
1. Cook pasta until al dente, then drain, reserving 1/2 cup pasta water.
2. In a skillet, heat olive oil and butter, then add garlic and cook until fragrant.
3. Add shrimp and cook until pink, seasoning with salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes.
4. Add lemon zest and juice to skillet, then toss in cooked pasta, adding reserved pasta water as needed to loosen.
5. Remove from heat, sprinkle with parsley, and serve hot, optionally garnished with Parmesan.

Enjoy your Lemon Shrimp Pasta!
